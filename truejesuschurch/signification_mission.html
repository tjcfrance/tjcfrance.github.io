What is the meaning of True Jesus Church ?

  • There is a special relationship that unites God, the True, and the Church, the assembly of elected through the Lord Jesus. In the book of Hebrews, the author claimed Jesus always intercedes for us (Heb 7.22-25).
  • The Lord Jesus is called the true vine (jn 15:1). The Church, which is His body, is thus called the true church because she's in accordance with the Truth. --John 15:5 "I am the vine, ye are the branches."
  • The true church : which conforms to the Truth, not preaching the True Jesus Church gospel, but bypreaching a complete gospel from word of God.



    • The evangelical mission

  • The mission of True Jesus Church is to revive the Church of apostolic times. She was born in Orient and fulfills her mission, from the East to the West.
  • And he said unto them, Go ye into all the world, and preach the gospel to every creature (Mark 16:15). We know there is a long way to go. We must pray and ask the Lord to guide us on this path, and help us to accomplish this mission he has entrusted to us.
