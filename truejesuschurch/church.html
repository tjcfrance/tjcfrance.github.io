Home The church

  • As a Church independent established through the Holy Spirit in 1917, True Jesus Church accomplishes the mission given by the Lord Jesus to preach the good news to all creation (Mark 16:15-16) and take care of the spiritual needs believers (John 21:15-17).
  • By preaching the Truth, the complete gospel, The Church is now present on all five continents. Jesus supported our testimony by signs, miracles and communication of the Holy Spirit (Heb. 2.4).
  • Prayer, mutual encouragement and studies of the scriptures are an integral part of life for members. Led by the Lord, True Jesus Church, the ark of the end of time, will continue to spread the Gospel for open the Salvation to all.
